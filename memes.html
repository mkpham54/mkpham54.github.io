memes mashup

In this meme mashup, I have tabled the tables and had Peppa discover someone in her chocolate. In the original trend within the TikTok community, a Peppa Pig figurine is found in obscure places and picked up by those asking the question,"Peppa...what are you doing here?" I used this opportunity to incorporate a choked Sasuke by editing him onto Peppa's arm, with her being the one asking why Sasuke is, in fact, in her chocolate.
